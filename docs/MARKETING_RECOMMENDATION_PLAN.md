# Nutritional App: "Marketing by Recommendation" Module Plan (Brazil Market)

## 1. Executive Summary
**Project Name**: *Ciclo de Ouro (Golden Circle)* - Automated Referral System
**Goal**: To empower Brazilian nutritionists (Nutris) to grow their private practice through automated, high-trust referrals ("boca a boca") leveraging AI, without requiring them to be salespeople or social media influencers.
**Core Concept**: Turn successful patient outcomes into automatic marketing assets.

## 2. Market Analysis: The Brazilian Nutri & "IndicaÃ§Ã£o" Culture

### The User Persona (The "Nutri")
- **Profile**: Highly technical, empathetic, cares deeply about patient health.
- **Pain Point**: Overwhelmed by administration. Hates "selling". Feels "fake" or "cringe" doing TikTok dances.
- **Financial Reality**: Often undercharges. Relies 90% on word-of-mouth but hopes it happens passively.
- **Brazil Specifics**:
    - **WhatsApp is the OS**: 99% of communication happens here. Email is for login resets only.
    - **Relational Culture**: Brazilians buy from people they trust.
    - **CFN Regulations**: Strict ethical codes (Conselho Federal de Nutricionistas) prohibit guaranteeing results (e.g., "lose 10kg in 1 week") and restrict "Before/After" images.

### The Opportunity
Most Nutris have happy patients but never ask for referrals because:
1.  They forget.
2.  They feel awkward/needy.
3.  They don't make it easy for the patient.

## 3. The "Ciclo de Ouro" Module: Features & AI leverage

This module is an add-on package that automates the Referral Loop.

### Core Features

#### A. The "Magic Moment" Detector (AI-Driven)
**How it works**: The system monitors patient data (weight logs, adherence check-ins, positive sentiment in chat).
**AI Trigger**: When a patient hits a milestone (e.g., "Lost 5% body weight" or " maintained habits for 30 days"), the AI identifies this as a **"Magic Moment"**.
**Action**: The system prompts the *Nutri* (or auto-acts, based on settings): *"Maria just hit her goal! Send her a congratulatory card + referral link?"*

#### B. The "Invisible" Ask (WhatsApp Integration)
Instead of a generic "Please refer me," the AI generates a customized message for the **Patient** to forward to *their* friends.
*   **Old Logic**: "Send my contact to your friends." (Too much work for the patient).
*   **New Logic**: The AI generates a "Brag Card" for the patient.
    *   *AI Draft*: "Check this out! I finally conquered my craving for sweets with Dr. Ana's help. She's opening 2 spots next week. DM her if you want the link!"
    *   **The Hook**: The patient shares to brag about *themselves*, the Nutri gets the exposure.

#### C. The "Voucher Amigo" (Referral Reward System)
**Mechanism**:
- Patient A refers Patient B.
- Patient B gets a "Special Assessment" (e.g., bioimpedance free).
- Patient A gets a "Maintenance Discount" or "Exclusive E-book" or "VIP Check-in".
- **AI Automation**: Tracks the codes/links and auto-rewards.

#### D. Content "Micro-Dose" Generator
**For the Nutri who hates Instagram**:
- The AI takes anonymized successes (e.g., "A 35y woman reversed pre-diabetes") and turns it into a *Case Study Story*.
- **Format**: Text optimized for WhatsApp Status (highly viewed in Brazil) and Instagram Stories.
- **Zero Effort**: Nutri clicks "Approve", and it posts/copies to clipboard.

### Profile 4: The "Introverted Nutri" (The Actual User)

**Critique Summary**: "I will never click 'Send'. It feels desperate."

**50-Point Improvement Plan**:
1.  **"Set and Forget"**: The system must operate 100% in the background. "Auto-pilot mode".
2.  **The "Third Party" Frame**: The message shouldn't come from "Me". It should come from "My Assistant" (The AI). "Hi, this is Dr. Ana's assistant..."
3.  **Positive Reinforcement**: Show the Nutri *compliments* received before asking them to do marketing.
4.  **Reframing**: Rename "Marketing" to "Helping More People". Change the psychological label.

5.  **Call to Action (CTA)**: clear commands. "Click here to book" not "Visit my site".
6.  **Urgency**: "Agenda opening for March" creates FOMO.
7.  **Storytelling**: Structure the "Magic Moment" as a Hero's Journey (Struggle -> Guide -> Victory).
8.  **Power Words**: Use Brazilian triggers: "Metabolismo", "Desintoxicar", "Definitivo", "Sem passar fome".
9.  **Format Variety**: Text, Bullet points, Short Questions. Don't be monotonous.
10. **A/B Testing**: The AI should subtly test two scripts and learn which one gets shared more.
